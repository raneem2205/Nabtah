Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I take care of my new plant?
A: Each plant comes with a detailed care guide, outlining light, water, and soil requirements. You can also find care tips on our website.
Q: Do you offer plant disease treatment services?
A: Yes, we provide specialized plant disease treatment services. Our experts will diagnose and treat your plants to ensure they regain their health.
Q: Can I order gardening tools and accessories online?
A: Absolutely! Our website features a wide variety of pots, seeds, insecticides, fertilizers, and other gardening essentials available for purchase online.
Q: How do I schedule a garden maintenance or landscape design consultation?
A: You can schedule an appointment by visiting our Services page or contacting us directly through the Contact Us page.
Q: What is your return policy?
A: We offer a flexible return policy on plants and products. Please refer to our Return Policy page for more details.
