About us

What is Nabtah? Nabtah is an Arabic word that means "plant". True to its name, we are an online shop offering a diverse range of indoor and outdoor plants. Our goal is to make your homes more dazzling and beautiful by providing various plant shopping options without the need to go out. Want to decorate your home, fill your garden with plants, or need plants for a special occasion? Just contact us, and we will ensure your needs are met. Our mission is to serve you, and by ordering from us, you help us grow and improve to serve you better! Why are we online? We're here to help you reach your loved ones, no matter where you are in the world. Pick the perfect gift, send it with just a couple of clicks, pay online, and that's it! Safe, fast, and easy!
